Cambridge Primary Check Point Prep


Stage 6

Duration: 1 year

This course is offered to learners of Stage 6. They are groomed in English, Maths and Science for the Primary Checkpoint Exam that takes place in mid-April every year.

The tests are marked in Cambridge and provide an external international benchmark for learner performance. Each learner receives a statement of achievement and a diagnostic feedback report, giving detailed information and feedback to the parents.

IGCSE 1

IGCSE is an academically challenging and balanced programme of education that prepares students for success at university and life beyond.The core curriculum is an overview of the subject and is suitable for students who are expected to achieve grades C to G.

The extended curriculum is like a specialisation in that subject. It is more challenging and designed for students who are expected to achieve grades A+ to C.

Learners of both core and extended curriculum are provided with after school programs in Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology,French/Hindi, Business Studies, Economics and English.

IGCSE 2

IGCSE is an academically challenging and balanced programme of education that prepares students for success at university and life beyond.The IGCSE learners in India have their Board attempt in Feb-March, though some schools follow the International attempt in May-June.

Learners are provided with after school programs in Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology,French/Hindi, Business Studies, Economics and English.We cater to learners appearing for both Feb and May attempts.
