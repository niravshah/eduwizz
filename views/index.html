What's unique about the
Eduwizz Approach?

At Eduwizz, we work with students from all the different IGCSE schools in Mumbai. So we are very well placed to understand their problems and offer a holistic solution to making sure learning is fun and effective for students.

Based on the years of experience gathered by our team of 15 IGCSE industry experts through coaching and student interaction, we think we have arrived at the ideal formulae for making students feel confident and well prepared for their curriculum and exams.

Students often tell us of a 'content-overload' - there's far too much unstructured, scattered and general purpose study material available. To add to the problem the application based testing style of International Boards is difficult to adapt for the learners.

All of this, together, leads to the learner just feeling confused instead of feeling well prepared for his/her exams. We have tried to address this problem by providing a one-stop solution at our center:

  • + Explanation by Experts
  • + Structured Topic-wise Study Material
  • + Topic –wise Tests
